🔧 MOST COMMON FIX (90% of cases)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Issue: Chart.js CDN is blocked by firewall/antivirus

  Solution: Download Chart.js locally

  Commands:
    mkdir static\js
    Invoke-WebRequest -Uri "https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/chart.js@4.4.1/dist/chart.umd.min.js" -OutFile "static\js\chart.min.js"

  Then edit templates/base.html line 14:
    Change: <script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/chart.js@4.4.1/dist/chart.umd.min.js"></script>
    To: <script src="{{ url_for('static', filename='js/chart.min.js') }}"></script>

  Restart Flask and hard refresh browser (Ctrl+Shift+R)
